How I confirm a conversion is a real enquiry

Platform conversion counts are a starting point, not the truth. Here is how I make sure the number in Google Ads reflects genuine calls and form enquiries, so the reporting you send your client is honest.

1
Track every ad-driven call

Google call reporting and call extensions, plus dynamic number insertion on the site where possible, so calls from ads are captured with time, duration and status.

2
Filter by call quality

Only answered calls of a meaningful length count as a genuine enquiry. Missed calls, wrong numbers and short misdials are excluded, not celebrated.

3
Validate form leads

Form submissions are set up as key events through Google Tag Manager and checked for spam or bot entries before they count.

4
Reconcile three sources

Google Ads, GA4 and the call log are cross-checked so the conversion number ties back to real logged enquiries, not an inflated platform figure.
